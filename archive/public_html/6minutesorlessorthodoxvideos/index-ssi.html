What are "SIX minutes of Less Orthodox videos? Research has shown that we moderns are very impatient people and will rarely watch a video all the way through that is longer than six minutes. We feel there is a great need to get information out there, and have it be WATCHED all the way through! These are mostly catechetical videos, with an emphasis on practical demonstrations.

Great Lent
How to pray the prayer of St Ephrem
A practical demonstration of how to hold your fingers when you make the sign of the cross, do a prostration and a low bow, and the long form of the prayer of St Ephrem. This prayer is said many times on the weekdays of Great Lent
