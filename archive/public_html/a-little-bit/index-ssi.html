A Little bit about the Orthodox Christian faith

The Orthodox Christian faith is full of life-giving truth, but it is also very complex and even overwhelming to beginners. It is not at all like the typical Western expression of Christianity, and this is part of the reason why it is so overwhelming and seems too big to many people. These small postings are in attempt to give “A Little Bit” of knowledge and edification regarding Orthodox Christianity. Since they come from my mind, they are not necessarily organized, but they are absolutely from the heart. It may be very hard to learn all the fasting rules and to go to long services and to read the Fathers, or even the Bible, but it is not so hard to read or listen to or watch a short video about something that is just “A Little Bit” about Christianity. Please send suggestions for other topics, and comments. Priest Seraphim Holland, (seraphim@orthodox.net) who is learning a little bit each day about how to be a real Christian.
