  • 6:30 PM: Bridegroom Matins

Holy Friday, March 31 / April 13 (April 13 is the civil date)

  • 3 PM - Vespers

Holy Saturday, April 1 / April 14 (April 14 is the civil date)

  • 10:30 AM - Hours

  • ~ 10 PM - Reading of the Acts
  • 11:30 PM – Midnight Office
  • 12 Midnight (Sunday Morning) – Paschal Matins, Divine Liturgy
  • 3:30 AM (Sunday morning) - Blessing of Baskets and feast

PASCHA, Sunday, April 2 / April 2 (April 2 is the civil date) NOTE: THERE IS NO DIVINE LITURGY ON SUNDAY AT THE USUAL MORNING TIME OF 10:00 AM! THE PASCHAL LITURGY WILL BE EARLY SUNDAY MORNING, FOLLOWING PASCHAL MATINS, WHICH BEGINS AT MIDNIGHT ON SUNDAY

  • 12 Midnight (Sunday) – Paschal Matins, Divine Liturgy
  • 3:30 AM (Sunday morning) - Blessing of Baskets and feast
  • 2:00 PM - Agape vespers, followed by Paschal feast at Pelagia's home
