Temple Address:

3617 Abrams, Dallas Texas, 75214-3009

We are in the Lakewood area of Dallas, on the West side of Abrams, a bit South of Mockingbirdю The church is on the campus of All Saints Episcopal, in white building behind their main building, and totally separate from it. There is an entrance off the alley, and a sign on the door.

Directions:

  1. Take 75 (Central Expressway)
  2. to Mockingbird. Mockingbird is about 5 miles south of 635.
  3. Turn East onto Mockingbird to Abrams. On the Northwest corner of Abrams and Mockingbird, there is a shopping center with a Tom Thumb grocery store, and on the SE corner, (which you will see if you look straight ahaead and to the right a little)
  4. there is a large "TCBY" (The World's best Yogurt)sign .
  5. Turn South onto Abrams.
  6. Head south on Abrams a short distance past the first stop light.
  7. You will see Thomas Aquinas Catholic Church on your right (West side). Go past this, and their football field.
  8. Immediately following is All Saints Episcopal, on the right (West) side of Abrams (3617 Abrams).
  9. Turn right at All Saints (there are two entrances), and find the alley.
  10. We have a separate building, with an entrance off the alley, and a sign on the door.
