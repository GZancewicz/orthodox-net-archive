Directions to St Nicholas Russian Orthodox Church

McKinney, Texas

Contact Information
Julian/Civil Calendar with schedule and readings.

Temple Address:

708 S Chestnut, McKinney, TX 75069-6503

Directions:

  1. Take US-75 North to McKinney
  2. Nearing McKinney, follow signs for TX-5 North
  3. After passing El Dorado St., you will soon see a large “Pawn Shop” sign on your left. Keep straight.
  4. Just after this sign, turn left at Christian Street.
  5. The Church is ahead on your right.
