Information for 2/20-21/2021, and forward

Updated: 2/19/2021 12:25:00 PM

 

We will have a normal service schedule this weekend. There will be substantial snow melting today and Saturday. The roads are passable.

 

We have moved the parish meeting to next weekend, Sunday, February 28, ns. It was planned for this coming weekend, but we decided that there may be people that are still digging out or are dealing with plumbing issues or some other thing that is distracting them or making them unable to come to the service.

 

We anticipate a normal service schedule from this point forward.

 

There may be some of you in our parish who have burst pipes. We have a benevolence fund, and will be collecting in mighty mites this weekend to add to it. If any of you that are regular parishioners have a financial need please contact for Fr Nicholas or father Seraphim. The benevolent fund is exactly for things like this.

Change! St. Nicholas Orthodox Church will be holding its annual parish meeting on Sunday, February 28th, 2021 at 12:00 p.m. in the parish hall. The meeting will convene in the presence of a quorum of 67% of the parish membership. If a quorum is lacking at 12:00, the meeting will convene one hour later regardless of the presence of a quorum, in accordance with the parish by-laws.

The agenda of the meeting is as follows:

    1. Pastor's report and vision for the parish in 2021
    2. election of the members of the Church Parish Council (Secretary, Treasurer and Council Members) and the Auditing committee
    3. examination of the report of the Church Parish Council on the activities of the parish during 2020;
    4. examination and ratification of the Church Warden's and Treasurer's reports;
    5. examination and ratification of the report of the Auditing Committee and the budget for 2021.
