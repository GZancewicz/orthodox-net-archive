The Apostol

Translated from the Slavonic

This book is based the Slavonic Apostol published by Holy Trinity Monastery (Jordanville, 1970). Additions to the Menaion section have been made for newer services.

The Book also contains Prokeimena, Alleluia, Communion Hymns, and Antiphons used throughtout the year, and numerous other explanatory, historical and reference material.

The PDF files are ready for offset printing, a project that we hope will be done soon. Some of the materials are also available in HTML format, as the hyperlinks show.

Please enjoy the texts provided, but do not print and bind them, distibute them in book form, or distribute them in any other electronic media (cd-rom, on the internet, etc), or sell them. All texts are copyrighted.

You can help make a truly complete and beautiful printed English Apostol a reality. If you find these texts to be useful and want to contribute to the printing of the Apostol, please contact us.
