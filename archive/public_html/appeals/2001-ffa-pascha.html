Оп Behalf of our First-Hierarch, His Eminence Metropolitan Vitaly, the Executive Committee and all donors ,  we greet you with the Holy Feast of Christ's Resurrection and extend our best wishes of good health and spiritual joy to all.

The Fund for Assistance is appealing for Pascha Donations

  • for the support of our monasteries and convents in the Holy Land and other countries
  • for the development of seminary education and missionary activity
  • for the dissemination of spiritual and community-related literature
  • for the support of Orthodox education among our children
  • for our participation in the spiritual revival of Russia
  • for the support of the Synod of Bishop's Chancery
  • for the support of newly-forming parishes throughout our diaspora
  • for the support of church-related endeavors

 

Please, also remember the Church in your will.
