Review
I would like to recommend to you the magazine Doxa, published by St. Michael's Skete, a branch of the Monastery of the Glorious Ascension. It is a well written, thoughtful magazine with articles that are interesting, as well as informative. Fr. Andrew is a very good writer, not just stating opinions, but backing them up with fact. In the latest issue there are articles entitled "The Language of Awe, The Veil and the Iconostasion", an article about the Western Rite, an article entitled "The Ambo" and a few other shorter items. It's quite good and I highly recommend it.

Matushka Doreen Bartholomew
