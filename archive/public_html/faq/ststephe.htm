The recorded hymns are chanted in the Byzantine tradition in celebration of the Nativity of our Lord and Savior Jesus Christ and are selected from the services of the Royal Hours, Vespers, Orthros and the Divine Liturgy of St. John Chrysostom. This celebration is offered by Chanter, Frank George of St. George Orthodox Cathedral, Worcester, Massachusetts and by his son , Reader Patapius Lloyd George of St. Stephen the Protomartyr Orthodox Church, South Plainfield, New Jersey in thanksgiving to God and for the benefit of the Building Fund of St. Stephen's.
