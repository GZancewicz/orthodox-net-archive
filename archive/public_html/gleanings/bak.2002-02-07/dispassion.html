Dispassion

2 entries

Dispassion does not mean that a man feels no passions, but that he does not accept any of them.

Saint Isaac of Syria

That soul has dispassion which is immersed in the virtues as the passionate are in pleasures.

St. John Climacus, "The Ladder of Divine Ascent," (Boston: Holy Transfiguration Monastery, 1978), Step29: Concerning Heaven on Earth, or Godlike Dispassion and Perfection, and the Resurrection of the Soul Before the General Resurrection
