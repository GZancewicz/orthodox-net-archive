Gleanings from Orthodox Christian Authors and the Holy Fathers

Chiliasm

This false teaching wreaks terrible harm, lulling to sleep the spiritual vigilance of the faithful and suggesting them that the end of the world is far away {if in fact there will be an end} ,and therefore there is no particular need to watch and pray,to which Christ the Saviour constantly called his followers {Mt.26-41} since everything in the world is gradually getting better and better spiritual progress keeping step with materialism.

Archbishop Averky ,ultimate things, by Dennis Engleman p116
