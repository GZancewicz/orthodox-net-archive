Glossolia

The multitude of the hearers was confounded; - it was a second confusion in the room of that first evil one at Babylon. For in that confusion of tongues there was division of purpose, because their thought was at enmity with God; but here minds were restored and united, because the object of interest was godly.

St. Cyril of Jerusalem, Catechetical Lectures (#17).
