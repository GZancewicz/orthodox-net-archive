Gleanings from Orthodox Christian Authors and the Holy Fathers

John 16:33

...He Who created us ... took on the form of being which He Himself had created, in order, as man, to manifest to us in our flesh the perfection of the Father to which we, too, are called. 'Be of good cheer; I have overcome the world' (John 16:33)."

Archimandrite Sophrony (His Life is Mine, Chapter 11; SVS Press pg.84)
