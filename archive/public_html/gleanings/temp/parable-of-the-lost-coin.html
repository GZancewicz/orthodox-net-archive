Gleanings from Orthodox Christian Authors and the Holy Fathers

Parable-of-the-lost-coin

In the parable of the woman who lost one of her coins a search then was made for that which had fallen, for which purpose the woman lighted a lamp (Lk. 15:8-10). For we were found, as I said, by the wisdom of God the Father, Which is the Son, when the divine and intellectual light shone upon us, and the sun arose, and the day star ascended and the day dawned (2 Pet. 1:19), according to the Scripture.

St. Cyril of Alexandria
