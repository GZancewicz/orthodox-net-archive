49. The paralytic's recovery, 50x40, studio "Kanon", Evgeni Majagin, 1997, wood, priming with chalk and glue, tempera, gilding. Church of St. Cosma and St. Damian in Moscow. This icon remembers one of the evangelical episodes of the terrestrial life of Jesus: the recovery of the paralytic who, miraculously healed, taken with himself his own bed, on which he had been brought in front of Christ and he went alone away.
