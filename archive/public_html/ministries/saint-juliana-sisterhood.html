St. Juliana of Lazarevo Sisterhood

 

July 2009

 

The St. Juliana of Lazarevo Sisterhood works hard to keep the church clean, prepare a weekly meal after the Divine Liturgy, rotates prophora baking duties among members, and maintain an online database of Orthodox recipes, among other tasks. The recipes can be accessed on the sisterhood blog.
