ROCOR Russian Orthodox Southern Missions Conference

November 9 -11, 2001
Orthodoxy & the Religion of the Future

With the blessing of Vladika Gabriel, this fall's Southern Missions Conference will be held at Lutheridge Retreat Center, Arden, North Carolina -- which is 5 minutes drive from St Nicholas Church, Fletcher.

COST

  • SINGLE occupancy:$225 (includes room, board, conference);
  • DOUBLE OCCUPANCY:$175 (includes room, board, conference);
  • Commuters & Teens: $125 (includes meals & conference for commuters room, board, conference for teens)
  • Commuter/No Meals: $50;
  • Commuter Teen: $50 (incl. meals, Youth Workshops);

Child Care: $25 (programs and/or meals).

[Child care available. Programs available for youth ages 6 -12. Youth Workshops are open to registrants aged 13 - 18.]

PRICE INCLUDES:

  • Two nights at Lutheridge Retreat Center (non-commuters)
  • Conference registration, materials, and shuttle service
  • Friday: refreshments
  • Saturday: breakfast, lunch & dinner
  • Sunday: lunch
