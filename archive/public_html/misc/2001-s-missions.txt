With the blessing of Vladika Gabriel, this fall's Southern Missions 
Conference will be held at Lutheridge Retreat Center, Arden, North
Carolina --  which is 5 minutes drive from St Nicholas Church, Fletcher.

<LI>Commuter/No Meals:  $50; 
<LI>Commuter Teen: $50 (incl. meals, Youth Workshops); 
</UL>

Child Care: $25 (programs and/or meals).
