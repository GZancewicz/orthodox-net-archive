Troparia

Hieromartyr Eutychius, Troparion in Tone IV —
As thou didst share in the ways of the apostles/ and didst occupy their throne,/ thou didst find thine activity to be a passage to divine vision,/ O divinely inspired one./ Wherefore, ordering the word of truth,/ thou didst suffer for the Faith even to the shedding of thy blood.// O hieromartyr Eutychius, entreat Christ God, that our souls be saved.

Kontakion, in Tone III, "Today the Virgin..." —
As one who shared the throne of the apostles and the adornment of holy hierarchs,/ O Eutychius, thou wast glorified in martyrdom,/ didst shine forth like the sun, illumining all,/ and didst dispel the profound night of ungodliness,// Wherefore, we honor thee as a truly divine initiate of the mysteries of Christ.
