Troparia

St. Poemen the Great, Troparion, in Tone VIII —
With the streams of thy tears thou didst irrigate the barren desert,/ and with sighs from the depths of thy soul thou didst render thy labors fruitful an hundredfold,/ and becamest a beacon for the whole world, resplendent with miracles.// O Poemen our father, entreat Christ God, that our souls be saved.

Kontakion, in Tone IV, 'Thou hast appeared..." —
Today the holy memory of thy splendid struggles hath dawned, O father,/ gladdening the souls of the pious,// O divinely wise Poemen, our venerable father.
