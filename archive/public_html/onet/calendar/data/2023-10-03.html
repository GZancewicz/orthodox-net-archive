Troparia

Holy Greatmartyr Eustathius and those with him, Troparion of the martyrs in Tone IV —
In their sufferings, O Lord,/ Thy martyrs received imperishable crowns from Thee our God;/ for, possessed of Thy might,/ they set at nought the tormentors and crushed the feeble audacity of the demons.// By their supplications save Thou our souls.

Kontakion of the martyrs in Tone II "Seeking the highest" —
Manifestly emulating the sufferings of Christ, / and having drained His cup with fervour, O Eustathius, / thou wast a partaker and fellow heir of glory,// receiving divine remission from on high from the God of all Himself.
