Troparia

Holy Martyr Callistratus and his companions, Troparion in Tone IV —
In their sufferings, O Lord,/ Thy martyrs received imperishable crowns from Thee our God;/ for, possessed of Thy might,/ they set at nought the tormentors and crushed the feeble audacity of the demons.// By their supplications save Thou our souls.

Kontakion in Tone IV, "Having been lifted up" —
Having endured multifarious wounds and manifestly received crowns from God,/ pray to Christ for us who celebrate your most festive memorial,/ O great Callistratus and ye blessed ones who suffered with him,/ that He may grant peace to His flock and people;// for He is the confirmation of the faithful!
