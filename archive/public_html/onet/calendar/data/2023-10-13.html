Troparia

Holy Hieromartyr Gregory of Greater Armenia, Troparion in Tone IV —
As thou didst share in the ways of the apostles/ and didst occupy their throne,/ thou didst find thine activity to be a passage to divine vision,/ O divinely inspired one./ Wherefore, ordering the word of truth,/ thou didst suffer for the Faith even to the shedding of thy blood.// O hieromartyr Gregory, entreat Christ God, that our souls be saved.

Kontakion in Tone II "The steadfast" —
O ye faithful, in songs and hymns/ let us all praise today the right glorious hierarch Gregory/ as an athlete, a vigilant shepherd and teacher,/ a universal lumi­nary and champion;// for he prayest to Christ/that we be saved.
