Troparia

Holy Wonderworkers and Unmercenary Physicians Cosmas & Damian, Troparion, Tone VIII —
O holy unmercenaries and wonderworkers Cosmas and Damian, visit our infirmi­ties:// Freely have ye received, freely give unto us!

Kontakion, Tone II "The steadfast" —
Having received the grace of healings,/ ye extend health to those in need,/ O all-glorious physicians and wonderworkers./ By your visitation cast down the audaci­ty of the enemy,// healing the world with miracles.
