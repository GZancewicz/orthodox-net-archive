Troparia

Holy Prophet Nahum, Troparion, Tone II —
Celebrating the memory/ of Thy prophet Nahum O Lord,// through him do we entreat Thee, save Thou our souls.

Kontakion, Tone IV "Thou hast appeared today" —
Thy pure heart, enlightened by the Spirit,/ became the dwelling-place of most splendid prophecy;/ for thou beholdest things afar off as though they were near./ Wherefore, we honor thee,// O most blessed and glorious Prophet Nahum.
