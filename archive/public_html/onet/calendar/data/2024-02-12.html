Troparia

Three Holy Hierarchs, Troparion, Tone IV —
In that ye share in the ways of the apostles, O teachers of the whole world, entreat the Master of all, that He grant peace to the world and great mercy to our souls.

Kontakion, Tone II, "The steadfast" —
The sacred and divinely eloquent preachers, the foremost among the teachers, O Lord, hast Thou received into the delight of Thy good things and into rest; for Thou hast accepted their pangs and labors as greater than any wholeburnt offer­ing, O Thou Who alone dost glorify Thy saints.
