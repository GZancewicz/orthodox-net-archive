Troparia

Meatfare or Relative's Suturday, Troparion, Tone VIII —
 O Thou Who with wisdom profound orderest all things with love, and Who givest to all what is needful, O only Creator, give rest, O Lord, to the souls of Thy servants, for on Thee they have set their hope, our Maker and Builder, and our God.

Kontakion. Tone VIII —
 With the Saints give rest, O Christ, to the souls of Thy servants, where there is no pain, no sorrow, no sighing, but life everlasting.

St. Tarasius, Archbishop of Constantinople, Troparion, Tone IV —
The truth of things revealed thee to thy flock as a rule of faith,/ a model of meekness and a teacher of abstinence/ wherefore thou hast attained the heights through humility/ and riches through poverty.// O hierarch Tarasius our father, entreat Christ God that our souls be saved.

Kontakion, Tone III "Today the Virgin" —
Having illumined the Church with Orthodox dogmas/ and taught all to honor and bow down before the precious icon of Christ, O blessed one,/ thou didst denounce the godless decree of the iconoclasts./ Wherefore, we cry out to thee:// Rejoice, O wise rather Tarasius!
