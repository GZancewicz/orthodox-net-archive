Kontakion, Tone VIII "To thee, the Champion Leader" —
O Sophronius of Jerusalem, most wise among patriarchs, who struggled with divine zeal,/ spread the commandments of truth with thy lips, set the founda­tions of the Church in good order,/ and there transmitted them to those in the monastic ranks:/ thou didst bring most wise discourses to light; and, instructed thereby, we cry out to thee:// Rejoice, O splendid boast of the Orthodox!
