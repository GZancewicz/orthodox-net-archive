  • General categories for prayer.
    Everyone has certain things that "resonate" with them. These are the things that resonate with me. The things that resonate with me and you may be different. This is my list. Make it your own, or make a different list, but, without fail, make a list! The world is full of terrible things, but also the beautiful One, Who abides in our hearts. We must pray about these terrible things, and in so doing, we emulate the Beautiful One.

  • Prayer of Holy Martyr Boniface, His Troparion, Kontakion, and Life.
    This is an excellent prayer to add to your daily prayers. We must remember our sins, and also that there are many martyrs that are suffering in our world, in such places such as Syria and Iraq. There are many other places where people who believe in Christ are suffering greatly. We must not forget them and must pray for them. Some of us may be called on by God to be witnesses (this is what the word "martyr" means), even with our blood. Through the prayers of the Holy Martyr Boniface, may God help us to have courage to live as true Christians.
