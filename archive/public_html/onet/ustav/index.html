Russian Orthodox Typicon (Ustav)

The Typicon (Greek: Τυπικόν, Slavonic: Устав) is the liturgical rule book that governs the celebration of divine services in the Orthodox Church. This section contains practical information about the Typicon, liturgical rubrics, and the ordering of services throughout the Church year.
