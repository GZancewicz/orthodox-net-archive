Priest: Let God arise and let His enemies be scattered!

Choir: Christ is risen from the dead, trampling down death by death, And on those in the tombs bestowing life.

Priest: As smoke vanisheth so let them vanish!

Choir: Christ is risen from the dead …

Priest: So let sinners perish at the presence of God and let the righteous be glad!

Choir Christ is risen from the dead …

Priest: This is the day which the Lord hath made; let us rejoice and be glad therein!

Choir: Christ is risen from the dead …

Priest: Glory to the Father and to the Son and to the Holy Spirit. Both now and ever and unto the ages of ages. Amen.

Choir: Christ is risen from the dead …

Clergy (with louder voice): Christ is risen from the dead, trampling down death by death …

Choir: And on those in the tombs bestowing life.
