    • We must make extraordinary effort when we are experiencing extraordinary things. About 7 min (mp3 format)
      • E
      2019-08-15
      SYNOPSIS:Reflections from a prison ministry visit. We must make extraordinary efforts when we are experiencing extraordinary things. This applies to prison, and all Christian life, anywhere. People have a tendency to say or even just think: "I need to do what I can to survive". They have a tendency to say "I’m under great stress so I deserve a break". Actually, when were under great stress, we must turn to prayer, and do other extraordinary things. This is one of the secrets to living a transcendent Christian life.
