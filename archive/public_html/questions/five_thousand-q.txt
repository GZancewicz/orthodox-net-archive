Questions about the Feeding of the Five Thousand 

QUESTION 1

QUESTION 2

QUESTION 3

QUESTION 4

QUESTION 5

QUESTION 6

QUESTION 7

QUESTION 8

There is an outer and inner meaning to the twelve baskets full. What is it?
