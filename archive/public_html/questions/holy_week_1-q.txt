Questions about holy week

QUESTION 1

Holy week has several beautiful services that have a name that reminds one of marriage. What is the 
name? When are they served? There  is a distinctive "signature" hymn that is sung. What is its text? What 
is the scriptural basis for the hymn?

QUESTION 2

How many Presanctified liturgies are served during Holy week? When is the last one? How is the service 
different than most of the ones served during Great lent?


QUESTION 3

does it unfortunately often replace?


QUESTION 4

When did Judas contact the Jewish officials to betray the Lord? The services make this clear. Was he 
present at the last supper? If so, what was he present for and what did he miss (if anything)?


QUESTION 5

QUESTION 6

did the Lord give him while he was on the cross? 


QUESTION 7

QUESTION 8

QUESTION 9
