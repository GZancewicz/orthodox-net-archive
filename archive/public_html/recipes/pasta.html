LENTEN RECIPES

Pasta

STIRFRY SPAGHETTI

Spaghetti and water to cook it

soy sauce

garlic powder

as assortment of veggies for stir frying, ie:

  • carrots
  • onions
  • cabbage
  • cauliflower
  • snow peas

You can use as many varieties as you want, totalling about 1/3 to 1/3 to amount of spaghetti you use.

While the spaghetti water is boiling and the spaghetti is cooking, peel, slice, etc the veggies you are using, and stir fry them in a little oil (water if you don't want to use oil) for a few minutes, leaving them still crisp. Stir them into your drained pasta, sprinkle with soy sauce and garlic.
