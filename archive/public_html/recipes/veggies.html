LENTEN RECIPES

Vegetable Dishes

BERT PIE (not a dessert)

2-3 cups left over cooked rice

1 10 oz pkg. frozen broccoli, thawed and drained

1 can stewed tomatoes

top and bottom pie crust

Combine the first three ingredients, place in bottom pie crust (in a pie pan). Top with top crust. Press edges, cut a couple slits in top pie crust. Bake at 350 about 30-40 min.

CABBAGE RICE

3 c chopped cabbage 1 c long grain rice

3 T tomato paste 2 c water

1/4 c oil 2 t chopped parsley

1 t salt 1/4 t pepper

Cook cabbage and seasonings in oil over low heat 10-15 min. Add water and tomato paste; bring to a boil. Add rice; reduce heat, cover and simmer until rice is light and fluffy.

BAKED BEANS

2 cans northern beans 1/2 c. fresh parsley

1/3 c oil 1 t sugar

1 8 oz can tomato paste 1 t salt

4 onions, chopped 1/4 t pepper

1/2 green pepper, chopped 1/3-1/2 c water

Sauté onions in oil until transparent. Add rest of ingredients except beans. Cook 5 min. Mix with beans. Bake, covered, at 325 for 40-60 min. They may be fairly dry - stir in a little water if necessary.

TOMATOED POTATOES

1/4 c chopped onion 1/4 t pepper

1/4 c margarine 1 can tomato soup

3 T flour 1/2 can water

2 T Worcestershire sauce 1/4 t garlic powder

1 t salt 5-6 potatoes, sliced

Sauté onions in margarine until tender. Blend in remaining ingredients except potatoes; cook and stir until thickened. Layer sliced potatoes in greased casserole dish. Cover with sauce. Bake at 350, covered, till done, about 45 min.

BAKED POTATOES

You already know how to bake potatoes; this is just a reminder that they work well during a fast. We top them with margarine, onions, green peppers, sesame seeds, raisins, etc.

BROWN RICE WITH VEGGIES

1 T vegetable oil 1/2 t rosemary

1/2 chopped onion 1 t salt

1 carrot, peeled and sliced 1 c brown rice

1 14.5 oz can Vegetable broth

(or from bouillon cubes)

1/2 c water

Sauté onion in oil until tender. Add rice and carrots, sauté for l minute, stirring often. Add broth, water, rosemary and salt; bring to a boil. Reduce heat, cover and simmer for 30 min. until liquid is absorbed.
