Petitions at the Great Entrance of the Divine Liturgy

In the Russian Orthodox Church outside of Russia

Also in PDF and RTF Versions Deacon: The Orthodox episcopate of the Russian Church; our lord the Very Most Reverend N., Metropolitan of Eastern America and New York, the First Hierarch of the Russian Church Abroad; and our Lord, the Most Reverend Laurus, Archbishop of Syracuse & Trinity, Deputy of the First Hierarch; and our lord the Most Reverend Bishop N., may the Lord God remember in His kingdom, always, now and ever, and unto the ages of ages. Priest: The much suffering Russian Land and its Orthodox people both in the homeland and in the diaspora; this land, its authorities and the faithful that dwell therein, may the Lord God remember in His kingdom, always, now and ever, and unto the ages of ages. The clergy, the monastics, all that are persecuted and suffer for the Orthodox Faith; the founders, benefactors, and the brotherhood of this holy temple (or holy monastery), and all of you Orthodox Christians, may the Lord God remember in His kingdom, always, now and ever, and unto the ages of ages.
