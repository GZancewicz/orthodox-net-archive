Divine Liturgy Hymns in English and Transliterated Slavonic

Litany

Gos-po-dee po-mee-looy.
Lord have mercy.

Po-day Gos-po-dee.
Grant this, O Lord.

Tye-bye Gos-po-dee.
To Thee, O Lord.

Ee doo-kho-vee Tvo-ye-moo
And to thy spirit. Trisagion

Svya-tuiy Bo-zhye, Svya-tuiy Kryep-kuiy, Svya-tuiy Byez-smyert-nuiy, po-mee-looy nas.
Holy God, Holy Mighty, Holy Immortal have mercy on us.

Sla-va Ot-tsoo, ee Sui-noo, ee Svya-to-moo Doo-khoo, ee nui-nye ee prees-no, ee vo vye-kee vye-kov. A-meen.
Glory…Both now… Cherubic Hymn

Ee-zhye khye-roo-vee-mui tay-no ob-ra-zoo-yu-shchye,
Let us who mystically represent the Cherubim

Ee zhee-vot-vo-rya-shchyey Tro-ee-tsye tree-svya-too-yu pyesn pree-pye-va-yu-shchye,
And chant the thrice-holy hymn unto the life-creating Trinity

Vsya-ko-ye nui-nye zhee-tyey-sko-ye ot-lo-zheem po-pye-chye-nee-ye.
Now lay aside all earthly cares.

A-meen. Ya-ko da Tsa-rya bsyekh po-dui-myem, an-gyel'-skee-mee
nye-bee-dee-mo dor-ee-no-see-ma cheen-mee. Al-lee-loo-ee-ya…

Amen. That we may receive the King of all Who comes invisibly upborne in triumph by the angelic hosts. Alleluia. Anaphora

Mee-lost mee-ra, zhyer-tvoo khva-lye-ni-ya
A mercy of peace, a sacrifice of praise.

Ee so-doo-khom tvo-eem.
And with thy spirit.

Ee-ma-mui ko Go-spo-doo.
We lift them up unto the Lord.

Dos-toy-no ee pra-byed-no yest po-kla-nya-tee-sya Ot-tsoo, ee Sui-noo, ee Cvya-to-moo Doo-khoo, Tro-ee-tsye ye-dee-no-sooshch-nyey ee nye-praz-dyel-nyey.
It is meet and right to worship the Father and the Son and the Holy Spirit, the Trinity one in essence and indivisible.

Svyat, svyat, svyat Gos-pod Sa-va-of, ees-poln nye-bo ee zyem-lya sla-vui tvo-ye-ya: o-san-na v'vuish-neekh, bla-go-slo-vyen gra-duiy vo ee-mya Gos-pod-nye, o-san-na v'vuish-neekh.
Holy, holy, holy Lord of Sabaoth, heaven and earth are full of Thy glory. Hosanna in the highest. Blessed is He that cometh in the name of the Lord. Hosanna in the highest.

Tye-bye po-yem, tye-bye bla-go-slo-veem, tye-bye bla-go-da-reem, Gos-po-dee, ee mo-leem-tee-sya, mo-leem-tee-sya, Bo-zhye nash, Bo-zhye nash, Bo-zhye nash.
We praise Thee, we bless Thee, we give thanks unto Thee, O Lord, and we pray unto Thee, pray unto Thee, O our God, O our God, O our God. It is truly meet Dos-toy-no-yest, ya-ko vo-ees-teen-noo bla-zhee-tee Tya, Bo-go-ro-dee-tsoo, Pree-sno-bla-zhye-noo-yu ee Prye-nye-po-roch-noo-yu ee Ma-ter Bo-ga na-shye-go. Chyest-nyey-shoo-yu Khye-roo-veem ee slav-nyey-shoo-yu byez srav-nye-nee-ya Sye-re-feem, byez ees-tlye-nee-ya Bo-ga Slo-va rozhd-shoo-yu, soo-shchoo-yu Bo-go-ro-dee-tsu Tya vye-lee-ch-yem.

It is truly meet to call thee blest, the Theotokos, ever blessed and all immaculate and the Mother of our God. More honorable than the Cherubim, and beyond compare more glorious than the Seraphim, thee who without corruption gavest birth to God the Word, the very Theotokos, thee do we magnify.
