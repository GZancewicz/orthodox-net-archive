Icons of Celtic Saints available via Orthodox News

Those who have a devotion to the saints of Britain and Ireland may be interested in the following list of icons, available in postcard form from St George Orthodox Information Service, The White House, Mettingham, Bungay, Suffolk NR35 1TP (Tel/Fax: 01986-896708)

  1. Christ with six Celtic saints;
  2. Mother of God with six Celtic saints;
  3. Holy Apostle Aristobulus, First Bishop of Britain;
  4. St Alban, Protomartyr of Britain;
  5. St Colman of Lindisfarne;
  6. St David of Wales;
  7. St Patrick of Ireland;
  8. St Brendan the Voyager;
  9. St Cuthbert of Lindisfarne;
  10. St Nectan of Hartland;
  11. St Cedd of Essex;
  12. St Oswin of Northumbria;
  13. St Columba of Iona;
  14. Sts. Comgall, Gall and Columbanus of Bangor Abbey;
  15. St Finan of Lindisfarne;
  16. St Eanswythe of Folkestone;
  17. St Felix of Dunwich;
  18. St Brihtwold of Ramsey;
  19. St Frideswide of Oxford;
  20. St Angus of Keld;
  21. St John of Beverley;
  22. St Aidan of Lindisfarne;
  23. St Brannock of Braunton;
  24. St Urith of Chittlehampton;
  25. St Endelienta of Cornwall;
  26. St Edmund of East Anglia;
  27. St Fursey of Burgh Castle;
  28. St Sidwell of Exeter;
  29. St Oswald of Northumbria;
  30. St Ita of Kileedy; and
  31. St Chad of Lichfield.

The icon cards cost 20 pence each, i.e. five cards for GBP1.
